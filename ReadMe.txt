
You must collect as many coins within the time limit, at the end of the game the highest score will be recorded inside the game. 
The longer that you survive, will add additional points to the score.

There will be telporters that the player will stand on that will take to the player to a separate area of the level. 

Controls:
You use the motion controls of the mouse to move the character.
Middle mouse button: jump
Left and right mouse buttons: Turn the camera
